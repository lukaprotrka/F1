What is Formula 1?

The Ultimate Racing

Formula 1 is the highest class of international racing for single-seater formula racing cars. It combines cutting-edge technology, skilled drivers, and strategic brilliance.

Speed & Innovation

F1 cars can reach speeds over 370 km/h and feature the most advanced automotive technology in the world, from hybrid power units to active aerodynamics.

Global Championship

The season spans multiple continents with races in iconic locations, from Monaco's streets to Monza's historic circuit, creating a truly global spectacle.

Beginner's Guide to F1

Race Weekend Format

Without Sprint

1

Practice Sessions

Three practice sessions (FP1, FP2, FP3) allow teams to test setups and gather data about tire performance and car balance.

2

Qualifying

Three knockout rounds (Q1, Q2, Q3) determine the starting grid. The fastest driver in Q3 earns pole position.

3

Race Day

The main event where drivers compete for points. Races are approximately 305 km (except Monaco at 260 km).

With Sprint (Sprint Qualifying + Sprint Race)

1

Practice Sessions

2

Sprint Qualifying (6 per season)

Three knockout rounds (SQ1, SQ2, SQ3) determine the starting grid. The fastest driver in SQ3 earns pole position for the sprint race. Held instead of FP2.

3

Sprint Race (6 per season)

A shorter race on Saturday, only ~100 km. Top 8 finishers get points. Held instead of FP3.

F1 Flags

Key Concepts

Overtake Mode

An overtaking aid replacing DRS from 2026. It gives the driver extra electric deployment and switches the car into a low‚Äëdrag aero state on straights, increasing acceleration and top speed. Activation is tied to energy limits rather than proximity rules or fixed zones.

Pit Stops

Strategic stops to change tires, make adjustments, or repair damage. Teams can change all four tires in under 2 seconds. Tire strategy is crucial for race outcomes.

Safety Car

Deployed during dangerous conditions to slow the field. Drivers must stay behind the Safety Car and cannot overtake. Often triggers strategic pit stop windows.
